Experience the Beauty of the West Coast

Avanti Guest Lodge

Old World Charm West Coast Accommodation

Discover serenity at our enchanting retreat in Vredenburg, surrounded by charming fishing villages and powered by sustainable solar energy, ensuring uninterrupted bliss without the hassle of load shedding. All our units are fitted with aircons to ensure maximum comfort. Immerse yourself in the warmth of old-world hospitality while enjoying modern amenities, including a swimming pool and organic vegetable garden.

Escape the hustle and bustle as you unwind in our spacious garden, complete with team-building activities like putt-putt and a chess area. Embrace relaxation amid the tranquil ambiance, far from the distractions of traffic and city life. Our West Coast accommodation gem offers a harmonious blend of comfort and coastal allure.

Experience the joy of camaraderie with friends and family with our cornhole area and oversized Jenga blocks, perfect for memorable gatherings. At our property, every moment is an opportunity to connect with nature and indulge in leisure.
