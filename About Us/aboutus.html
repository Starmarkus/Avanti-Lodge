About Us

Avanti Lodge


Discover Avanti Guest Lodge, a hidden gem conveniently nestled in the heart of Vredenburg, surrounded by retail shops and banks, all within easy walking distance. Indulge in our 4 spacious, en-suite rooms adorned with a dining room, a lounge featuring a fireplace, and a fully equipped kitchen. Additionally, we provide 4 self-catering units complete with a dining area, kitchenette, and en-suite facilities. Benefit from seamless connectivity with free WiFi throughout the property, while your vehicle finds a secure spot in our complimentary, on-site parking.

Experience entertainment at its finest with Smart TVs featuring selected DSTV channels for your viewing pleasure. Avanti Guest Lodge stands just 500m away from Life West Coast Private Hospital and Vredenburg State Hospital, catering to the medical needs of the West Coast community.

For those seeking relaxation, take a refreshing dip in our newly installed swimming pool on hot summer days. The property ensures your safety with full electric fencing, an alarm system, access-controlled on-site parking, and 24-hour CCTV surveillance. Your tranquil and secure haven awaits at Avanti Guest Lodge.
