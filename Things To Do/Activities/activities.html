ADVENTURES AT AVANTI

A Blend of Old World Charm and Modern Vibes

Introductory paragraph describing the purpose and mood of the activities offered.

GARDEN CHESS

Unveil the strategic mastermind within you at our captivating garden chess area. Nestled in our peaceful garden, this enchanting outdoor space invites guests to indulge in leisurely matches while surrounded by the tranquility of nature. Whether you’re a seasoned player or new to the game, our garden chess area offers the perfect setting to sharpen your skills or enjoy a friendly competition with friends and family.

Immerse yourself in the serene ambiance as you strategise your next move, surrounded by the big trees and soothing sounds of the garden. This idyllic retreat not only provides a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life but also fosters a sense of connection and camaraderie among players.

With its blend of natural beauty and intellectual stimulation, our garden chess area is more than just a recreational space—it’s an opportunity to unwind, socialise, and engage in the timeless game of chess amidst the splendor of the outdoors.

PUTT-PUTT

Tee off for a day of fun and friendly competition at our exciting putt-putt course. Nestled within the serene surroundings of our lodge, our miniature golf course offers a delightful experience for guests of all ages.

Immerse yourself in the challenge of navigating through the whimsical obstacles and tricky turns of our meticulously designed course. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or new to the game, our putt-putt course provides the perfect opportunity to test your skills and enjoy quality time with friends and family.

Step onto the green and let the friendly rivalry begin as you aim for that elusive hole-in-one. With its charming setting and engaging gameplay, our putt-putt course is sure to provide hours of entertainment and laughter for everyone.

So grab your putter, gather your group, and embark on a memorable putt-putt adventure at our lodge.

CORNHOLE

Experience the classic American pastime of cornhole in a picturesque setting at our lodge. Set against the backdrop of lush artificial grass, our cornhole area provides the perfect space for guests to enjoy this beloved lawn game.

Gather your friends and family and test your tossing skills as you aim to land your bean bags on the board and score points. Whether you’re a seasoned player or new to the game, cornhole offers endless entertainment for all ages and skill levels.

As you play, take in the scenic surroundings and enjoy the camaraderie of friendly competition. With its laid-back atmosphere and simple rules, cornhole is the perfect activity for relaxing and unwinding during your stay at our lodge.

So grab your bean bags and get ready for a memorable afternoon of fun and laughter at our cornhole area.

JENGA

Get ready for towering fun with our giant Jenga blocks at the lodge! Located in a vibrant outdoor space, our oversized Jenga game adds excitement to any gathering.

Challenge your friends and family to a test of steady hands and strategic moves as you carefully remove blocks from the towering structure without causing it to tumble. With each block removed, the tension mounts, making every move crucial and every victory sweeter.

Whether you’re enjoying a laid-back afternoon with loved ones or hosting a lively event, our giant Jenga blocks promise hours of entertainment and laughter. Plus, the durable construction ensures that the fun can continue game after game.

So gather your group, head to our outdoor area, and prepare for an unforgettable experience with our giant Jenga blocks at the lodge.
